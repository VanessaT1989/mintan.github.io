-- Introduce --

Min Tan is a New Media Technology student from LaGuardia Community College, and has a strong background in digital art and design, as well as artistic innovation. As an immigrant from China, Min's artwork incorporates elements of Chinese art and traditional Chinese culture, which makes her work different from other artists. Min embark on a journey that goes beyond aesthetics, delving into the core of user experience, problem-solving, and innovation. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for pushing boundaries, design majors are at the forefront of transforming ideas into tangible, visually captivating, and purposeful expressions.
